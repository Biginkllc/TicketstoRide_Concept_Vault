# NFT Layer (Optional Enhancement)

## Overview

The TicketstoRide concept includes an optional NFT-based layer designed to enhance group travel experiences through digital artifacts and engagement tools.

This layer is not required for implementation and is presented as a forward-looking enhancement.

---

## Potential Use Cases

- **Digital Boarding Passes**  
  NFT-based passes representing group travel bookings or special events

- **Collectible Travel Artifacts**  
  Unique, branded items associated with specific trips, destinations, or experiences

- **Group Identity Tokens**  
  Digital identifiers for travel groups, entourages, or coordinated bookings

---

## Characteristics

- Customizable and brandable
- Transferable between users (optional)
- Compatible with existing digital wallet ecosystems
- Designed for promotional or engagement purposes

---

## Strategic Role

The NFT layer serves as:

- A **differentiation tool** for airlines and travel platforms
- A **marketing and engagement mechanism**
- A **future-facing extension** of the TicketstoRide concept

---

## Positioning

This layer is presented as an **optional enhancement** only.

No technical implementation, blockchain infrastructure, or regulatory claims are included.

Adoption and execution would be determined by the partnering entity.
