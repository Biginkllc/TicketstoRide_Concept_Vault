# Domain Cluster List

This document outlines the current TicketstoRide domain holdings and their intended roles within the concept structure.

---

## Core Domains (Primary Use)

- ticketstoride.travel  
  *Industry-facing entry point for travel and airline positioning*

- ticketstoridegroup.com  
  *Group travel layer for entourages, events, and coordinated bookings*

---

## Web3 Domains (Expansion Layer)

- ticketstoride.crypto  
- ticketstoride.web3  
- ticketstoride.onchain  
- ticketstoride.unstoppable  
- ticketstoride.south  

*These domains represent the blockchain-based identity and future expansion layer of the TicketstoRide concept.*

---

## Supporting Web2 Domains (Extended Coverage)

- ticketstoride.info  
- ticketstoride.net  
- ticketstoride.online  
- ticketstoride.pw  

*These domains provide extended brand coverage across traditional web channels.*

---

## Notes

This domain cluster is intended to be presented as a **multi-layered naming and branding system**, not as isolated domain assets.

Additional domains may be added or refined as the concept evolves.
